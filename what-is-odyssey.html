What is Odyssey of the Mind?

Odyssey of the Mind is an international creative problem-solving competition that challenges students to think outside the box, work as a team, and present innovative solutions in fun and engaging ways.

Creativity

Teams solve open-ended problems that require out-of-the-box thinking. From building contraptions to performing skits, Odyssey encourages imagination without limits.

Competition

Teams present their solutions at regional, state, and even international competitions. Each solution is unique, showcasing the diversity of approaches to the same challenge.

Teamwork

Students collaborate, share ideas, and bring their talents together. No single person can do it all — success comes from the group’s synergy.

Global Community

Odyssey isn’t just local — students connect with peers worldwide who are passionate about problem-solving, innovation, and creativity.
